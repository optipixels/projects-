About Us

Company Profile

Welcome to Cliff View Custom Homes. We are excited and proud to introduce you to our Firm and to show you the great value that we will provide you. Howard Metersky is one of partners and he works very closely with each and every client to help them realize their dreams, whether it be a custom home or a luxury remodel.

Howard has accomplished a lot in his career and was second in command of a highly respected custom home builder and ran day to day operations. He acted as the client liaison, prepared proposals and making sure that the job went according to plan. He also worked with the architects and sub-contractors, bring his clients dreams to life. Howard helped build some of the finest homes in the Dallas Fort Worth Metroplex. Striving to realize his dreams and his vision, Howard along with his business partner, Neil Medwed, started Cliff View Custom Homes. CVCH builds luxury homes and performs luxury remodels in the Dallas – Fort Worth Marketplace, and beyond,

Neil Medwed, the other partner in our Firm, is a 33 year technology veteran and has run Preferred Technology Solutions for over 22 years. He has a great reputation in the technology industry and has been very involved nationally in leading the technology communities. Neil brings many things to the table including business expertise and technology knowledge. Having the right technology in your home is growing in importance and because of Neil’s background, Cliff View Custom Homes will bring the knowledge and expertise few others can provide.

A builder is only as good as its people and our staff has built a solid reputation as one of the best Luxury Home Builders in Texas with their exceptional quality, innovative ideas, and a commitment to project details that is unparalleled. We believe that first and foremost, it is important to have an honest and collaborative relationship with our clients. From the first meeting through to the final walkthrough, we make you our priority.
